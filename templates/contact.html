Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

Response times depend on the type of inquiry. Platform support questions are typically answered within 24 hours, legal information questions within 48 hours, and general inquiries within 72 hours.

Can you provide legal advice for my specific case?

Good Parents Justice provides legal information and tools, but we cannot provide specific legal advice for individual cases. For personalized legal advice, please consult with a qualified family law attorney.

Do you offer phone support?

Currently, we provide support primarily through email to ensure accurate and documented responses. For urgent technical issues, please email support@goodparentsjustice.ca and mark your message as "urgent."

Is my personal information kept confidential?

Absolutely. We maintain strict confidentiality and privacy standards. Your personal information is never shared with third parties and is protected with industry-standard security measures.

What if I need help outside of business hours?

Our AI assistant is available 24/7 through the platform for basic questions and guidance. For crisis situations, please contact the emergency resources listed above.
